    About

    Zed Attack Proxy began as a fork of Paros Proxy by Simon Bennetts. The 1st Release of ZAP was in September 2010. It was adopted by OWASP in October 2010 and is an OWASP Flagship project. ZAP continues to evolve and improve - thanks to the skills of a dedicated community of international volunteers. With over 50 years combined experience in information security and software development, the ZAP core team is passionate about web application and software security. To this end, ZAP is open source and free for everybody forever.
